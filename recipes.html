Spaghetti Bolognese

Ingredients:

  • 300g spaghetti
  • 400g ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano & basil
  • Salt & pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Grated parmesan

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti until al dente, drain.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil until soft.
  3. Add beef and cook until browned.
  4. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, simmer 20 mins.
  5. Season, serve over spaghetti with parmesan.

Simmer slowly for richer flavor.

Classic Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 200g digestive biscuits
  • 100g butter, melted
  • 500g cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps:

  1. Crush biscuits and mix with melted butter.
  2. Press mixture into a pan, chill 30 mins.
  3. Beat cream cheese with sugar until smooth.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mix in vanilla.
  5. Pour mixture over crust.
  6. Bake at 170°C for 45 mins, cool slowly.
  7. Refrigerate 2 hours before serving.

Chef Tip: Avoid overmixing to prevent cracks.

Grilled Chicken

Ingredients:

Prep Time: 15 mins

Cook Time: 20 mins

Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless chicken breasts
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix olive oil, garlic, paprika, thyme, salt, pepper, and lemon juice.
  2. Coat chicken thoroughly and marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  4. Grill chicken for 6–8 minutes per side until fully cooked.
  5. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Hawaiian Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • 150g ham, sliced
  • 100g pineapple chunks
  • 150g mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce ½ cup
  • Olive oil 1 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 220°C.
  2. Roll out dough, brush with olive oil and tomato sauce.
  3. Evenly distribute ham and pineapple.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella on top.
  5. Bake 12–15 mins until crust golden and cheese melted.
  6. Slice and serve hot.

Chef Tip: Use fresh pineapple for better sweetness.

Steak

Ingredients:

  • 2 ribeye steaks
  • Salt & cracked black pepper
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • Fresh thyme sprigs

Steps:

  1. Bring steaks to room temperature for 30 mins.
  2. Season generously with salt and pepper.

Garlic Butter Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 500g peeled shrimp
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped

Steps:

  1. Melt butter in a pan over medium heat.

Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 200g arborio rice
  • 200g mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 750ml chicken stock
  • 50g parmesan
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Olive oil 1 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil, sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  2. Add mushrooms and cook 5 mins.
  3. Add rice, toast 2 mins.
  4. Gradually add warm stock, stirring constantly.
  5. Cook until rice is creamy and al dente.
  6. Stir in butter and parmesan, serve immediately.

Chef Tip: Stir constantly for creamy risotto.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 200g flour
  • 200g sugar
  • 100g cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 100ml milk
  • 100ml vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, cocoa, and baking powder.
  3. Add eggs, milk, and oil, beat until smooth.
  4. Pour into greased pan and bake 30–35 mins.
  5. Cool completely before frosting.

Chef Tip: Use high-quality cocoa for richer taste.

beef lasagna

Prep Time: 25 mins

Cook Time: 45 mins

Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • ½ cup grated parmesan
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté onion and garlic.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer 10 minutes.
  5. Layer sauce, lasagna sheets, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  6. Repeat layers and finish with cheese on top.
  7. Bake 40–45 minutes until golden.
  8. Rest 10 minutes before slicing.

Vegetable Lasagna

>

Prep Time: 20 mins

Cook Time: 40 mins

Serves: 6

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 1 zucchini (sliced)
  • 1 red bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1 cup mushrooms (sliced)
  • 2 cups spinach
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup grated parmesan
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat olive oil and sauté zucchini, peppers, and mushrooms.
  3. Add spinach and cook until wilted.
  4. Spread tomato sauce in a baking dish.
  5. Layer lasagna sheets, vegetables, ricotta, and mozzarella.
  6. Repeat layers and finish with mozzarella and parmesan.
  7. Bake for 35–40 minutes until golden and bubbly.
  8. Let rest 10 minutes before serving.

Chef’s Tip

Add a sprinkle of fresh basil on top before serving for a premium restaurant finish.

Beef Bourguignon

Classic French slow-cooked beef in rich red wine sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef chuck (cubed)
  • 2 carrots (sliced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 cups red wine
  • 1 cup beef stock
  • 150g mushrooms
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt & black pepper
  • Fresh thyme

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Season beef with salt and pepper.
  2. Sear beef in butter until browned. Remove and set aside.
  3. Sauté onions, carrots, and garlic in the same pot.
  4. Add flour and tomato paste. Stir well.
  5. Pour in red wine and beef stock. Stir to combine.
  6. Return beef to the pot. Add thyme.
  7. Cover and simmer for 2–3 hours until tender.
  8. Add mushrooms in the last 30 minutes.
  9. Serve hot with mashed potatoes or crusty bread.

Chicken Stew

Ingredients:

  • 500g chicken pieces (thighs or drumsticks)
  • 2 medium carrots, diced
  • 2 medium potatoes, diced
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • Salt & pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Sauté onion and garlic until fragrant and translucent.
  3. Add chicken pieces, season with salt, pepper, thyme, and paprika, and brown lightly on all sides.
  4. Add carrots and potatoes, stirring for 2–3 minutes.
  5. Pour in chicken stock and bring to a gentle boil.
  6. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 40–45 minutes until chicken is cooked and vegetables are tender.
  7. Adjust seasoning and serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.

Chef Tip: Browning the chicken first enhances the stew’s depth of flavor.

  • 4 cloves garlic, minced

  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1 tsp paprika (optional)

Steps:

Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • Spices: cumin, turmeric, garam masala, chili powder
  • Fresh coriander and mint
  • 3 tbsp ghee or oil

Steps:

  1. Wash rice until water runs clear, soak 30 mins.
  2. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices for 1 hour.
  3. Fry onions in ghee until golden, set aside.
  4. Cook chicken until almost done, remove from heat.
  5. Boil rice until 70% cooked.
  6. Layer chicken and rice in a heavy-bottomed pot, sprinkle fried onions, coriander, and mint.
  7. Cover tightly, cook on low heat 20–25 mins (dum method).

Chef Tip: Add a few saffron strands soaked in warm milk for aroma and color.

Fish and Chips

Ingredients:

  • 400g white fish fillets (cod or haddock)
  • 100g flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 egg
  • 150ml cold beer or sparkling water
  • Potatoes for fries
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Cut potatoes into fries, soak 30 mins, then pat dry.
  2. Mix flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt.
  3. Whisk egg with beer until smooth, combine with flour to make batter.
  4. Heat oil to 180°C (350°F). Fry potatoes until golden, set aside.
  5. Dip fish in batter, carefully lower into hot oil, fry 4–5 mins until crispy.
  6. Serve fish with fries and tartar sauce.

Chef Tip: Keep beer or water very cold for a lighter, crispier batter.

Egg Curry

Ingredients:

  • 6 boiled eggs
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt, chili powder to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil, fresh coriander leaves

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a pan, sauté onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste, cook 1-2 mins.
  3. Add turmeric, cumin, coriander, chili powder, stir for 30 secs.
  4. Add tomato puree, cook until oil separates.
  5. Gently add boiled eggs, coat them in the sauce.
  6. Simmer 5-7 mins, garnish with coriander.

Chef Tip: Prick eggs slightly before simmering to absorb flavors.

Seared Salmon

Ingredients:

  • 2 salmon fillets
  • Salt and black pepper
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Lemon wedges for garnish

Steps:

  1. Pat salmon dry, season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a non-stick pan over medium-high heat.
  3. Place salmon skin-side down, sear 4-5 mins.
  4. Flip carefully, add butter, baste salmon 1-2 mins.
  5. Remove from pan, rest 2 mins, serve with lemon wedges.

Chef Tip: High heat ensures a crispy skin while keeping the inside tender.

Ingredients:

  • 300g pasta (fettuccine or spaghetti)
  • 200g mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 50g parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil, salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook pasta al dente according to package instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add mushrooms, cook 5 mins until soft.
  4. Pour in cream, simmer 3-4 mins, season with salt & pepper.

  6. Serve immediately, garnish with extra parmesan or parsley.

Chef Tip: Reserve ½ cup pasta water to adjust sauce consistency.

Chicken Quesadillas

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1/2 cup onions, diced
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Sour cream & salsa for serving

Steps:

  1. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Sauté onions and bell peppers until soft.
  3. Add shredded chicken, season with salt and pepper, cook 2-3 mins.
  4. Place a tortilla in a clean skillet, sprinkle cheese, then chicken mixture, top with more cheese.
  5. Cover with another tortilla, cook 2-3 mins per side until golden and cheese melts.
  6. Slice into wedges, serve with sour cream and salsa.

Use a mix of cheeses like cheddar and Monterey Jack for extra flavor.

Herb-Crusted Lamb Chops

Ingredients:

  • 8 lamb chops
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tsp rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tsp thyme, chopped
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (390°F).
  2. Mix garlic, rosemary, thyme, salt, pepper, and olive oil to make a marinade.
  3. Rub lamb chops with Dijon mustard (optional) and the herb mixture.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat, sear lamb chops 2–3 mins per side.
  5. Transfer to oven, bake 5–8 mins for medium-rare.
  6. Remove, rest 5 mins before serving.

Use fresh herbs for a fragrant and elegant flavor.
