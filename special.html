Classic Beef Lasagna

Rich, layered, and comforting — click below to reveal the full recipe!

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt & black pepper to taste
  • 9 lasagna sheets
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • ½ cup grated parmesan

Instructions

  1. Sauté onion and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer 20 minutes.
  4. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
  5. Layer baking dish: meat sauce, lasagna sheets, ricotta, mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  6. Top with mozzarella and parmesan.
  7. Bake 35–40 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  8. Rest 10 minutes before slicing.

Serving Suggestion

Serve with a fresh green salad and warm garlic bread for a restaurant-quality experience at home.
